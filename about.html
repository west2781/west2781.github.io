About Me

Turning data into insight, strategy, and impact.

Hi, I’m Lucas West — a data-driven problem solver with a passion for turning complex information into clear insights.

I’m currently earning both my Bachelor’s in Management Information Systems and Master’s in Management Information and Technology at the University of Oklahoma. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve developed a strong foundation in data science, analytics, and engineering—skills I’ve put into practice through internships and hands-on projects that span industries like energy, healthcare, sports, and retail.

Whether I’m building regression models with Python, designing databases in SQL Server, automating workflows in Alteryx, or creating interactive dashboards in Power BI and Tableau, I bring a focus on clarity, scalability, and impact. I thrive on making data useful — and usable.

I’ve presented work to leadership boards, developed dashboards from multimillion-row datasets, and built applications that solve real-world business problems. My goal is to apply data skills in a way that drives strategy, efficiency, and innovation.

Thanks for visiting my site — I hope you enjoy exploring my work!
