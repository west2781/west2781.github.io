Devon Energy - Tax Technologist Intern

I contribute directly to the optimization of tax reporting processes, the integrity of enterprise data systems, and the modernization of internal workflows. My role supports Devon’s Indirect Tax team, where I’ve had the opportunity to make meaningful improvements to how critical oil and gas data is handled.

  • Workflow Documentation: Authored clear documentation for automation pipelines and tax workflows to support long-term use and audits.
